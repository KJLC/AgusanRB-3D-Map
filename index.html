3D Visualization Platform of the Agusan River Basin

This interactive geospatial diorama highlights the work of PAGASA through its Flood Forecasting and Warning Center in the CARAGA Region. It presents the network of synoptic and hydrometeorological stations together with the X-band radar, all overlaid on a realistic 3D terrain. By combining science and visualization, the platform demonstrates PAGASAs vital role in monitoring rivers and strengthening flood forecasting and early warning for the Agusan River Basin.
